About Unified Security

Unified Security & Facility Management Services Pvt. Ltd. is a multi-domain, integrated facility management company, delivering world-class security, facility maintenance, horticulture, and manpower solutions.

Built on trust, discipline, and operational excellence, we ensure your business environments are safe, hygienic, sustainable, and productivity-optimized. We have evolved from a manpower-supply company into a full-spectrum facility management provider, combining human expertise with cutting-edge technology.

Our operations span across 25+ cities with a workforce of 1000+ skilled professionals serving 200+ satisfied clients.
